Review of John Wick

Jamaal Dublin Jr.

January 31st 2023

John Wick is about an ex-hit-man that has his puppy killed and car stole in an home evasion at the start of the movie and he's forced to come out of retirement and hunt down the men who robbed him for revenge because the puppy that they killed was a gift from his wife that had recently died and it was her last gift to him. The reason I enjoy the movie is because of the fighting scenes and the thrill throughtout it, while he's searching for the men he has a huge bounty put on his head that was sent to everyone in the city he's in which makes the film even more exciting and suspenseful.

Trivia

  • According to Keanu Reeves, he did 90% of his own stunts in the film.
  • Bacon grease was placed on Keanu Reeves' face to attract his dog to jump on him when he wakes up in bed.
  • Keanu Reeves' preparation for the role included weapons and martial arts training (eight hours per day for four months).
